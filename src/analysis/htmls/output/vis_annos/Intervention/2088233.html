A randomized , double - blind , placebo_2 -_2 controlled_1 study on the immediate clinical and microbiological efficacy of doxycycline_6 ( 100 mg for 14 days ) was carried out to determine the benefit of adjunctive_1 medication_1 in 16 patients with localized juvenile periodontitis .
