Comparable efficacy between the compounds was found across the 13-week study . Doxepin differed from bupropion mainly on the sleep factor of the Hamilton Depression Scale , with doxepin improving sleep to a greater extent than bupropion . Doxepin produced a greater incidence of anticholinergic side effects , including dry mouth , constipation , sleepiness , and tiredness , in comparison to bupropion . Also , increased appetite and weight gain were consistent side effects of doxepin relative to bupropion .
