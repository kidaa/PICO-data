A comparison of different antipyretics in children_2 with_2 malaria_2 showed a small effect of naproxen , but not of metamizol , on the reduction of fever peaks . Antipyretic treatment had no effect on fever clearance and therefore should be used cautiously in the treatment of malaria_2 ._2
