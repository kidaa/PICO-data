METHODS AND RESULTS The effects of a continuous 4-hour infusion of hBNP were determined in 16_2 decompensated_2 heart_2 failure_2 patients_2 in an invasive , randomized , double - blind , placebo - controlled study . Patients were evaluated during three 4-hour study periods : baseline , treatment ( placebo [ n = 4 ] versus hBNP 0.025 or 0.05 microgram / kg / min [ n = 12 ] ) , and post - treatment . Urinary volume losses were replaced hourly to separate the vasodilatory and diuretic effects of hBNP .
