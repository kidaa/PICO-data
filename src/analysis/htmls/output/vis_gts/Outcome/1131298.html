Effect of the essential oils of garlic and onion on alimentary hyperlipemia .

SUMMARY The effect of garlic and onion on alimentary hyperlipemia , induced by feeding 100 g butter , has been studied in 10 healthy subjects . The freshly extracted juice of 50 g of garlic or onion , as well as an equivalent amount of their ether - extracted essential oils , was administered randomly on four different days during a one - week period .
