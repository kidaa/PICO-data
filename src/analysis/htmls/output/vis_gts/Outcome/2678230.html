[ The effect of ionic and nonionic x - ray contrast media on myocardial perfusion and myocardial function ] .

The influence of injections of Amidotrizoate and Iopromide into the left coronary artery on global_1 myocardial_1 perfusion_1 and_1 function_1 ( pressures in the left ventricle , heart rate ) as well as excitation formation and transmission was studied in 16 patients with ischaemic heart disease . No statistically significant differences in myocardial_3 perfusion_3 measured were found with xenon-133 in this small group between rest and dipyridamole load for ionic and non - ionic contrast media .
